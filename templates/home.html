Gastric Precancerous Diseases Classification App

A web application that will be used for medical and health purposes, the app is a platform for physicians.

Upload Images

Physicians can upload an endoscopy image in the system. Inorder to predict or classify the lesions visible in the image.

Classify Images

After the image is uploaded the physician can press the button to predict or classify the lesions visible in the system.

Generate Report

After system displays it’s prediction the physician can generate the classification report in PDF format.

About

The services that will be provided to the physicians will be

  • Classification of gastric precancerous diseases.
  • Managing patients by adding and generating patient’s records.
  • Sharing those records with patients.
